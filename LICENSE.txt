SPEAK ANYWHERE v1.0.0

A voice-powered productivity application that provides:

  - VOICE DICTATION: Tap the microphone and speak - your words are typed
    automatically wherever your cursor is. Perfect for writing emails,
    documents, messages, and more without touching the keyboard.

  - TEXT-TO-SPEECH: Copy any text to your clipboard, then tap "Speak Clipboard"
    to have it read aloud using natural-sounding Microsoft neural voices.
    Great for proofreading, accessibility, or multitasking.

  - SPEED CONTROL: Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 2.0x for comfortable
    listening.

  - DEVICE SELECTION: Choose your preferred microphone and speaker from the
    dropdown menus.

REQUIREMENTS:
  - Windows 10 or Windows 11
  - Microphone (built-in or USB)
  - Speakers or headphones

HOW TO USE:
  1. Run SpeakAnywhere.exe
  2. Wait for the splash screen to finish loading
  3. Tap the microphone icon to start dictating
  4. Copy text and tap "Speak Clipboard" to hear it read aloud
  5. Use the X button to close the app
